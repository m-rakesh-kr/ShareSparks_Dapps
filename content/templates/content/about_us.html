About ShareSparks

ShareSparks is a semi-centralized social media platform that connects people from all over the world in a safe and secure environment.Creators can post their content and the audience may interact with the content as per their choice and the creators would get rewards in the form of Spark Tokens as per their content engagement.

ShareSparks combines the scalability and user-friendly features of centralized systems with the privacy and transparency benefits of decentralized systems.

The platform is designed to be user-centric, ensuring that users have complete control over their data

About Spark Tokens

Spark Tokens are the digital tokens used to facilitate transactions and interactions in the Sharesparks ecosystem.

They are the native cryptocurrency of ShareSparks platform.
